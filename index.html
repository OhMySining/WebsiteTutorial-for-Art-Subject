About Arts

MAPEH Art introduces students to the vibrant creative traditions and cultural identity of the Philippines through the study of local crafts. By exploring the unique elements of design found in Luzon’s highlands and lowlands, learners understand how history and natural resources shape artistic expression. This subject ultimately encourages a deep appreciation for heritage while empowering students to apply traditional techniques to their own creative projects. Learn More.
