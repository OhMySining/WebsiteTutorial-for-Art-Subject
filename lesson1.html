The Artistry of Luzon: From Highlands to Lowlands

Luzon's artistry spans from the indigenous, functional crafts of the Cordillera highlands to the Spanish-influenced, intricate designs of the lowlands.

Key traditions include highland weaving and woodcarving, alongside lowland pottery and more.

About this lesson

This lesson is a cultural journey across the largest island in the Philippines, exploring the distinct creative identities of the Highlands (the mountainous Cordillera region) and the Lowlands (coastal and agricultural provinces like Ilocos, Laguna, and Rizal). It focuses on how art is not just a decorative object, but a functional record of a community's history, environment, and beliefs.
